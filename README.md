A modern, feature-rich reimagining of the classic browser T-Rex runner. Built from scratch using **React** and **HTML5 Canvas**, featuring a custom physics engine, an in-game economy, unlockable achievements, and a beautiful glassmorphism UI.

## ✨ Features

* 🌌 **Immersive Environment:** A dynamic starry night background with parallax scrolling.
* 🛒 **In-Game Store & Economy:** Collect coins during your runs to buy premium skins (Neon, Magma, Cyber Hacker, etc.).
* 🎨 **Procedural Skin Patterns:** Dynamic Canvas rendering for gradients, stripes, grids, and checkerboards.
* 🏆 **Achievement System:** Complete specific goals (e.g., *Make 500 jumps*, *Score 10,000 points*) to unlock exclusive skins.
* 💾 **Persistent Progression:** All high scores, coins, and skins are safely saved locally.
* 📱 **Responsive UI:** Slick Glassmorphism design that transitions into a fullscreen gameplay experience.
